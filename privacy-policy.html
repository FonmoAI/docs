Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for using FonmoAI! This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, why it is collected, and how it is used when you interact with the FonmoAI bot on Discord.

3. Why We Collect This Data

The language settings are collected to ensure the bot functions correctly and provides responses in the appropriate language. This helps improve user experience and ensures compatibility with the server's preferences.

4. How We Use This Data

The collected data is used solely for the following purpose:

We do not share, sell, or disclose this data to any third parties.

5. Data Storage

The collected language settings are stored securely.
